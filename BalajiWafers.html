Business Details

Distributorship Application Accepting from Only : Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Karnataka, Telangana, Delhi, NCR, Bihar, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal, Jharkand


We, Balaji Wafers Pvt. Ltd., manufacture food products like Potato Wafers, Namkeen, Extruded Snacks, Noodles, Pellets, Aloo Sev, Gippi Noodles, Wheelos, Flamin' Hot, and similar products. Marketed under the brand name Balaji Wafers & Namkeen, our products are known for their crunchiness and mouthwatering taste. We care about the health of our customers and due to this our workforce prepares these food items using quality assured food ingredients. The offered range of namkeen is a great compliment to every beverage especially tea and cold drink. Our noodles are easy to cook and suitable for this fast-paced lifestyle.



We are one of the largest snack manufacturers in our country and we have the direct distribution presence in more than 10 states and we have 1300 distributors on our side. Thanks to our fully automated world-class plants across, we are able to ensure that our highest quality products are available to consumers in less time. We currently offer 50+ products that are available in 100+ SKU's.



We are also one of the oldest firms in this sector as we were established in 1982. The annual sale of our Rajkot, Gujarat, India based firm is INR 5000.00 Cr - 5500.00 Cr, which shows us as a successful food manufacturing company.



To increase our market reach in the north ( Haryana, Uttar Pradesh), central (Chhattisgarh), and south (Karnataka, Telangana) India, we are looking for city-level distributors.





Our range of snacks:
- Potato Wafers
- Namkeen
- Extruded Snacks

- Aloo Sev

- Flamin' Hot

- Chocolate

Please note that the above-mentioned list of products is not exhaustive.



USPs of our range of snacks:

- No harmful taste enhancers
